About Nackademin

Nackademin is a Vocational university which focuses on having educational programs related to what the job market is in need of right now. To always be up to date on that matter they have several companies on the bord who can keep the school updated on what’s going on in that particular field.

All teachers at Nackademin are actually consultants who also work with what they are teaching. In that way we learn techniques that are really up to date and useful for what we’re going to do when we graduate.

Internships are really important part of studying at Nackademin because that’s usually how you get a job after you graduate. We start thinking about it and preparing from day one. The school provide opportunities for students to meet with both companies on the bord and others to give us the best chances to get a job after graduation.
